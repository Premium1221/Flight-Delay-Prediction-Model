About Flight Delay Predictor

Flight Delay Predictor is a machine learning application that helps predict whether your flight will be delayed based on historical patterns and current flight information.

This application uses a model trained on thousands of flight records to make predictions about potential delays.

How it works:
  1. Enter your flight details in the form
  2. Our model analyzes the information
  3. You receive a prediction about potential delays
